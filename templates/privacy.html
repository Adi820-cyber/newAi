Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring transparency in how we handle it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, profile photo (if applicable), and any identifiers you provide.
  • Login Credentials: Encrypted passwords and tokens using industry-standard hashing and authentication methods.
  • Chat and App Usage Data: We store chat messages, activity logs, preferences, and interaction history to personalize your experience.
  • Device and Technical Data: Including browser type, IP address, operating system, device type, and crash logs for security and troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to:

  • Provide personalized features and AI responses
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts
  • Analyze usage trends and improve our platform
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Prevent unauthorized access or fraudulent activity
  • Scan and analyze URLs for security threats
  • Generate security reports and recommendations

3. Data Sharing and Storage

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

Your data is stored securely in Supabase, a GDPR-compliant backend-as-a-service platform.

Data may be accessed by our technical team only for maintenance, debugging, or improvement purposes, under strict confidentiality.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

4. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, update, or delete your data
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools to enhance your experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

Our analytics help us understand how users interact with our application, allowing us to improve functionality and user experience.

6. Security Analyzer Features

Our application includes security analysis features that scan URLs for potential threats. When you use these features:

  • We collect and analyze the URLs you submit
  • We store scan results to provide historical data and improve our detection algorithms
  • We may use anonymized scan data to improve our security detection capabilities

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All updates will be posted within the app or on our official website. We will notify you of any material changes by email or through a notice on our application.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related concerns, you may contact us at: support@securityanalyzer.com
